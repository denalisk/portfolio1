About Me

I am an epicodus student aspiring to become a professional developer, new enough to the field of programming that most things hold fresh magic.

I graduated from Whitman College in 2014, and have spent all my summers since working on wildland fire crews in Colorado and Oregon. Wildland firefighting is seasonal work, so I spent my winters travelling, skiing, backpacking, and trying to teach myself skills that would provide more consistent employment than just being good with a chainsaw and shovel. I stumbled across coding, and taught myself a bit of python before deciding that programming was a field I would enjoy pursuing further.

    Some of my favorite things, in no particular order:
  1. Outdoor endeavors
  2. Literary pursuits
  3. Biking through traffic
  4. Games of all sorts

I'm incredibly excited for Epicodus and so far have been blown away by the pace of learning and retention through the program.
